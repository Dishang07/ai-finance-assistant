Investment Options & How They Work

📈 Stock Market Investments
✅ Stocks (Equities)

Buying shares makes you a partial owner of a company. Potential for high returns but with high risk.

✅ Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs)

Basket of stocks tracking an index. Offers diversification with moderate risk.

✅ Mutual Funds

Professionally managed funds investing in stocks, bonds, or both. Risk varies by fund type.

💰 Fixed Income Investments
✅ Government Bonds

Safe investments with fixed interest payments.

✅ Corporate Bonds

Loans to companies with periodic interest payments.

✅ Fixed Deposits (FDs) & Certificates of Deposit (CDs)

Low-risk bank deposits with fixed returns.

🏡 Real Estate Investments
✅ Rental Properties

Invest in properties to earn rental income and capital appreciation.

✅ Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)

Companies owning income-generating real estate. Offers liquidity and steady returns.

🪙 Alternative Investments
✅ Gold & Precious Metals

Safe-haven assets that retain value over time.

✅ Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum)

Highly volatile digital assets with high-risk, high-reward potential.

🔀 Hybrid & Miscellaneous Investments
✅ Index Funds

Passive investment tracking stock market indexes with low fees.

✅ P2P Lending

Investors lend money to borrowers via online platforms for high-interest returns.

✅ High-Yield Savings Accounts

Safe and liquid savings options with better interest rates.

📊 Choosing the Right Investment

🔹 **Risk Tolerance:** Low, Moderate, High

🔹 **Investment Period:** Short-term or Long-term

🔹 **Goals:** Growth, Passive Income, Stability

Recommended Portfolio Allocation:
  • 📈 40% Stocks/ETFs
  • 💰 30% Bonds/Fixed Income
  • 🏡 15% Real Estate/Gold
  • 🪙 10% Cryptocurrency
  • 💳 5% High-Yield Savings
